The Beatrice Public Library

Mission:


The Beatrice Public Library is a nurturing environment that strives to provide unfettered access to necessary resources for self-improvement, cultural awareness, and pure enjoyment for patrons. We encourage life-long learning for all.



Vision:


BPL is a warm comfortable, welcoming, user-friendly place for people of all ages. It is available to the entire community, providing free guidance, cultural events, and access to technology, books, and media. We are to help you become the best version of you, that you have always imagined.
